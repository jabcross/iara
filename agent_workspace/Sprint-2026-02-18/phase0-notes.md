## Key Facts to Remember
- Degridder: degridding step of radio astronomy pipeline (sky image → visibilities)
- Comparison against Preesm implementation by Anaëlle Cloarec (WAMCA article)
- SEP tool converts .fits images to CSV — the regenerated CSVs cause the segfault
- Original inputs that worked were lost
- Runtime has two implementations: ring-buffer (older) and virtual-fifo (current)
- EnkiTS is the work-stealing backend in runtime/common/
- Compilation modes: baseline (sequential, omp-parallel-for, omp-task, enkits) vs IaRa (vf-sequential, vf-omp, vf-enkits)
- Testing framework: tools/experiment_framework/ with experiments.yaml per application
